1. Baked Chicken Tenders




These crunchy baked chicken tenders are life changing!
The secret to truly golden, truly crunchy baked breaded chicken is to toast the breadcrumbs in the oven first.

2. Pizza Scrolls



This easy pizza scroll recipe is made with fluffy homemade pizza dough, rolled thin and filled with cheese, pizza sauce, bacon and vegetables.
Perfect for lunchbox treats or a handy grab-and-go lunch, these delicious savoury scrolls can be customised with your favourite toppings and made in your oven or air fryer.
They make an irresistible treat that you will want to make again and again!

3. Pizza Quessadila



A pizza quesadilla, or "pizzadilla," is a fusion dish combining pizza toppings-typically mozzarella, sauce, and pepperoni-inside a folded, crispy flour tortilla. It is a quick snack or meal, usually prepared in a skillet in under 15 minutes by melting cheese on both sides to seal the ingredients.

4. Pasta Toss



A simple pasta toss is a quick, versatile dish made by combining cooked pasta with olive oil, garlic, fresh vegetables, and parmesan cheese.
It is designed to be a light, fast meal (often 20 minutes) where ingredients are lightly sauted and tossed together, allowing for easy substitutions like chicken, shrimp, or various vegetables

5. Chorizo and Egg Burritos



Chorizo and egg burritos are a popular, hearty breakfast dish featuring spicy Mexican sausage (chorizo) scrambled with eggs, typically wrapped in a flour tortilla with cheese, potatoes, and beans. They are known for being a savory, high-protein meal often toasted in a skillet to make them crispy.

6. Baked Pesto Chicken



Baked pesto chicken is a simple, high-protein dish featuring chicken breasts or thighs slathered in basil pesto and baked until tender and juicy.
It often includes toppings like mozzarella cheese, tomatoes, or breadcrumbs for texture.
It is a popular 30-minute weeknight meal commonly served with pasta, rice, or roasted vegetables
